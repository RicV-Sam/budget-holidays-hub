cheap holidays spain september

Last updated: April 2026

💡 Quick Answer

September is one of the best-value times for Spain: sea temperatures stay warm, school-holiday peak pricing drops, and package inventory often falls 12-25% versus August.

If you are searching for cheap holidays Spain September deals from the UK, focus on the second and third weeks of the month. You still get beach weather in most costas, but prices soften quickly once peak family demand ends. For bigger-picture planning, use our main Spain holiday guide.

Cheapest Months for Spain Trips

September is a strong shoulder-season month, but it is not the absolute cheapest. Typical value order for beach holidays is May, early June, late September, then October.

  • Best overall value: May and early June
  • Best weather-to-price balance: September
  • Lowest hotel-only rates: Late October in many resorts

Flights from UK to Spain in September

Best fare consistency usually comes from London Stansted, London Gatwick, Manchester, Bristol, and Birmingham. Tuesday or Wednesday departures are often cheapest, and early-morning outbound flights can cut total package price.

Best Destinations and Areas for September Value

  • Costa Blanca (Benidorm, Alicante): Strong late-season competition keeps prices low.
  • Costa Dorada (Salou, Cambrils): Family-friendly hotels discount after August.
  • Costa del Sol (Torremolinos, Benalmadena): Reliable weather and broad package choice.
  • Canary Islands (Tenerife south): Usually pricier than mainland Spain but stable weather.

Budget Tips That Actually Work

  • Set fare alerts 8-10 weeks out, then book when price dips below your target.
  • Compare seven-night and ten-night options; ten nights can be better value per night in September.
  • Choose half board only if you will eat onsite most nights; local menu del dia can be cheaper.
  • Check airport transfer costs before booking a "cheap" hotel outside main hubs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is September cheaper than August for Spain holidays?

Yes. In most UK departure markets, September prices are lower once school-holiday demand ends.

How warm is Spain in September?

Mainland coastal areas often sit around 24-30C in early September and remain beach-friendly through much of the month.

When should I book Spain September deals?

For best value, start tracking prices around June and book 6-10 weeks before departure.

Which part of Spain is cheapest in September?

Costa Blanca frequently leads on total package value because of high flight and hotel supply.
