Cheap Holidays To Kos From The UK

Last updated: March 2026

  • Daily budget: £42–£77
  • Cheapest month: May
  • Flights from UK: £75–£165 return
  • Best budget area: Kardamena / Mastichari
  • Top budget tip: Rent a bicycle; Kos is incredibly flat and perfect for exploring by pedal power.

Exploring Kos on a budget is not only possible but incredibly rewarding for savvy travellers from the UK. Whether your heart is set on witnessing the ancient Asklepion, exploring the depths of the historic Knights' Castle, or experiencing the thermal springs of Embros Thermae, Kos offers excellent value. If you enjoy Greek islands, you might also like budget travel in Corfu . The accessibility provided by a wide range of airline partners means that finding a deal to Kos is often just a matter of timing and a bit of research.

Once you've touched down, Kos reveals its true value through its affordable infrastructure and local charm. The availability of beautiful cycle paths and crystal-clear Aegean beaches ensures that every day is filled with wonder, regardless of your daily spend. By engaging with the local culture and choosing to stay in traditional neighbourhoods, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for Kos while enjoying a holiday that is as affordable as it is unforgettable.

Cheapest Time To Visit Kos

The cheapest months to visit Kos from the UK are:

  • May
  • October

These months offer the best balance of warm weather and low costs, especially for last-minute bookings.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Leeds Bradford

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Kos is very flat—rent a bicycle as a cheap and healthy way to get around
  • Visit the free thermal springs at Embros Therme
  • Eat at local tavernas away from the main harbour in Kos Town
  • Look for 'all-in' deals in Kardamena for great package value

Best Areas to Stay in Kos for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Kos doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like the bustling Kos Town, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like the resort hub of Kardamena and the peaceful Tigaki are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Kos while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Kos on a strict budget.
