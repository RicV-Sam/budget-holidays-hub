Cheapest Time To Visit Zante

The cheapest months to visit Zante from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September

Early summer is particularly pleasant as the island is lush and the prices are at their most competitive.

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book a boat trip to Shipwreck Cove from a local harbor for better prices
  • Stay in self-catering accommodation and shop at local bakeries
  • Rent a small car or quad bike to explore the quieter northern coast
  • Avoid the main tourist strip restaurants for better value and more authentic food

Best Areas to Stay in Zante for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Zante doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like the bustling Zante Town, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like the lively Laganas and the family-friendly Tsilivi are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Zante while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Zante on a strict budget.
