Cheapest Time To Visit Bulgaria

The cheapest months to visit Bulgaria from the UK are:

  • June and September (Beach)
  • January and March (Ski)

Avoid the peak school holiday weeks in August and February for the best deals.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Look for all-inclusive packages which are exceptionally cheap here
  • Use the local buses to travel between Burgas, Sunny Beach, and Nessebar
  • Drink local Bulgarian wine and beer for the best value
  • Book ski hire and lift passes in advance online

Best Areas to Stay in Bulgaria for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Bulgaria doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like the resort hub of Sunny Beach, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like affordable city stays in Sofia and the cultural old town of Plovdiv are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Bulgaria while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Bulgaria on a strict budget.
