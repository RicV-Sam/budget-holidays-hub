Last Minute Holiday Deals Strategy

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Last minute holidays can offer excellent savings, but only if you understand how the travel industry prices unsold inventory. If you're looking for inspiration on where to go, check out our guide to budget holiday destinations from the UK.

When Last Minute Deals Work Best

  • Outside peak school holiday periods
  • For flexible travellers
  • For short haul destinations

Understanding the cheapest time to book flights can also help you decide whether to wait for a last-minute deal or book in advance.

When To Avoid Last Minute Booking

  • School summer holidays
  • Christmas and New Year
  • Major events or festivals

Best Places To Find Last Minute Deals

  • Airline direct websites
  • Package holiday providers

Frequently Asked Questions

Are last-minute holidays cheaper than booking in advance?

Yes, last-minute deals can offer savings of 30–50% if you are flexible with your destination and departure dates.

How much can I save by booking a last-minute holiday?

Flexible travellers can often save between £100 and £300 per person compared to booking months in advance.

Which UK airports have the most last-minute holiday availability?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes for last-minute holidays.

How close to departure should I book a last-minute deal?

The sweet spot for last-minute deals is usually 2–4 weeks before departure, when prices are slashed to fill remaining capacity.

Which destinations are best for last-minute budget holidays?

Popular destinations like Spain, Turkey, and Greece typically have the best last-minute package availability from the UK.
