Cheapest Time To Visit Las Vegas

The cheapest months to visit Las Vegas from the UK are:

  • January and February
  • July and August (very hot)
  • November

Midweek stays (Sunday to Thursday) are always significantly cheaper than weekends.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Always stay midweek to avoid huge weekend price hikes
  • Watch out for 'Resort Fees' which are added to the daily hotel rate
  • Buy drinks at a CVS or Walgreens on the Strip rather than in the casinos
  • Join players' clubs for free slot play and dining discounts

Best Areas to Stay in Las Vegas for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Las Vegas doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like the affordable North Strip, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like historic Downtown/Fremont and the residential Henderson are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Las Vegas while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Las Vegas on a strict budget.
