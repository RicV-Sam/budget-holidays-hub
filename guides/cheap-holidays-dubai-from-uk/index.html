Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Ride the Abra: Skip the expensive boat tours and cross Dubai Creek on a traditional wooden "Abra" for just 1 AED (around 22p). It's the most authentic and affordable experience in the city.
  • Eat in Bur Dubai & Deira: Head to these historic districts for world-class Indian, Pakistani, and Middle Eastern street food that costs a fraction of mall or hotel prices.
  • Use the Dubai Metro: Avoid expensive taxis and use the driverless Metro. It's fully air-conditioned and connects the airport, Downtown, and the Marina for just a few Dirhams.
  • Enjoy Free Beach Days: You don't need a beach club. Kite Beach and JBR Open Beach offer pristine sand, great facilities, and views of the Burj Al Arab for free.

Best Areas to Stay in Dubai for Budget Travellers

To find the best value, look away from the immediate beachfront and consider these areas:

  • Deira/Bur Dubai: The historic heart of the city offers the highest concentration of budget-friendly hotels, authentic souks, and incredibly cheap local dining.
  • Al Barsha: Centrally located between Downtown and the Marina, this area offers modern hotels at much lower prices and is perfectly connected by the Metro.
  • Dubai Silicon Oasis: Slightly further out but offers fantastic value for money with high-quality, modern apartments and great local amenities.
