cheap holidays spain june

Last updated: April 2026

💡 Quick Answer

June is often the sweet spot for cheap holidays in Spain: summer temperatures arrive, but prices are usually below July and August. Early June is normally the cheapest week.

Cheap holidays Spain June searches are rising for a reason. You can still get pool-weather conditions across most Spanish coasts without paying full peak-season rates. If you want a broader country comparison first, start with our cheap holidays to Spain from the UK guide, then compare cheap holidays to Spain in September for shoulder-season pricing.

Cheapest Months to Book Spain

June is competitive on value, but the absolute cheapest Spain months are usually May and parts of October. June offers better weather consistency, so cost per "good weather day" is excellent.

For many travellers, cheap holidays to spain in may are the lowest-cost pre-summer option, while cheap holidays to spain in october can win on hotel deals once demand drops.

  • Lowest total prices: May and October
  • Best weather/value trade-off: June
  • Most expensive period: Late July through August

Best Destinations and Areas in June

  • Benidorm and Alicante: Strong package inventory and regular low-cost flights.
  • Malaga and Torremolinos: Good for short-haul sunshine with broad hotel choice.
  • Costa de Almeria: Often overlooked and can undercut better-known costas, especially if you are comparing the cheapest places to holiday in Spain.
  • Canary Islands: Stable weather, though sometimes pricier than mainland in June.

Budget Tips for June Spain Deals

  • Target departures in the first half of June before school-break demand rises.
  • Search one-bag cabin-only fares first, then add bags only if needed.
  • Choose resorts with walkable beaches to avoid repeated taxi costs.
  • Check Sunday returns too; many travellers avoid them, creating occasional discounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is June a good month for cheap holidays in Spain?

Yes, especially early June. It typically combines summer weather with lower pricing than peak season.

Is Spain cheaper in June or September?

Both can be strong value months. June can be cheaper on flights, while September can be cheaper on hotels after peak season.

How far in advance should I book June Spain holidays?

Aim for 6-12 weeks in advance for the widest choice and stable pricing.

Which Spanish coast is best value in June?

Costa Blanca often leads on overall value, with Costa de Almeria also worth checking.

Where can I find the cheapest Spanish resorts in early summer?

Costa Blanca and parts of Costa de Almeria are usually among the cheapest Spanish resorts in June when flight supply is high.
