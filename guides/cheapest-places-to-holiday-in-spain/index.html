cheapest places to holiday in spain

Last updated: April 2026

💡 Quick Answer

Costa Blanca is usually the cheapest place to holiday in Spain from the UK, followed by Costa de Almeria and Costa Dorada. High flight volume and dense hotel supply drive lower total trip cost.

The cheapest places to holiday in Spain are not always the most famous ones. This guide ranks value by total trip cost, not just headline flight prices. For route-level planning, read our main Spain from UK guide, then compare timing pages for cheap holidays to Spain in June and cheap holidays to Spain in September.

Cheapest Months Across Spanish Destinations

Even the cheapest Spanish resorts become expensive in peak weeks. For most destinations, these timing patterns hold:

If you are tracking cheap holidays to spain in may versus cheap holidays to spain in october, May often wins on flight pricing while October can win on accommodation discounts.

  • Lowest cost months: May and October
  • Strong shoulder season: June and September
  • Highest cost: Late July and August

Flights from UK: Where Savings Usually Start

The cheapest Spain routes are commonly from London Stansted, Manchester, Birmingham, and Bristol into Alicante, Malaga, and Reus. A cheap place with one-stop flights can still lose out against a direct route to a slightly pricier resort.

Best Budget Destinations and Areas in Spain

  1. Costa Blanca (Benidorm, Alicante): Usually the top value option for combined flight + hotel cost.
  2. Costa de Almeria (Roquetas de Mar): Lower hotel rates and less peak-season pressure.
  3. Costa Dorada (Salou): Competitive family pricing outside school-break peaks.
  4. Murcia (Los Alcazares): Often cheaper local food and quieter shoulder-season deals.

Budget Tips for Choosing the Cheapest Place

  • Rank destinations by total seven-night cost, not flight price alone.
  • Check transfer time and cost; long private transfers can destroy savings.
  • Use map search for accommodation to stay near beach and supermarkets.
  • Price weekday departures first, then compare weekend options only after.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the cheapest place to holiday in Spain from the UK?

Costa Blanca is usually the cheapest overall for UK travellers when looking at full trip cost.

Is southern Spain cheaper than the Canary Islands?

Mainland southern Spain is often cheaper on flights and hotels, though prices vary by date and resort.

Are city breaks or beach resorts cheaper in Spain?

Budget beach resorts often beat city breaks on accommodation value for longer stays.

What is the cheapest way to holiday in Spain?

Travel in shoulder season, fly midweek, and choose apartments near local supermarkets and public transport.
