Cheap Holidays to Greece from the UK (2026 Guide)

Last updated: March 2026

💡 Quick Verdict: Is Greece Worth It?

Yes. Greece offers a more traditional and rustic value experience than Spain or Turkey. While flights can be slightly more expensive, the "on-the-ground" cost of living in traditional Greek villages remains remarkably low. It is the top choice for authentic culture and island-hopping variety.

Navigating Greece on a budget in 2026 is all about island selection and timing. From the massive value of Crete to the hidden gems of the Peloponnese, your pound can stretch surprisingly far if you avoid the "Instagram hubs." Top 3 Value Regions in Greece

  • Crete: The "Value King." Its size ensures constant competition for flights and accommodation, and it produces its own food, keeping taverna prices grounded. Read Crete Guide.
  • Rhodes: Exceptional for high-quality, mid-range family all-inclusive deals. Read Rhodes Guide.
  • The Peloponnese: A mainland secret with island-quality beaches and significantly lower costs for car hire and local dining.

Strategic Value: When to Book

Cheapest Months: May and October. These "shoulder" months offer 25°C weather but hotel rates that are often 40-50% lower than August.

UK Airport Strategy: Gatwick and Stansted have the highest frequency, but Manchester and Birmingham offer excellent Jet2 package value to the larger islands.

Who is Greece Best For?

Greece is the Top Choice for Couples seeking romantic views without the price tag of Italy, and for DIY Travellers who enjoy "Studio Living" and authentic village life.

Greece vs Turkey

In 2026, Turkey is generally cheaper for 5-star all-inclusive luxury, while Greece offers a superior "local" experience for those who prefer to eat out at independent tavernas. See our Greece vs Turkey comparison for the full data.

Better or Cheaper Alternatives?

  • Albania: Often 30% cheaper than the neighbouring Greek Ionian coast for similar beaches. Read Albania Guide.
  • Cyprus: Offers more reliable sun in late November when the Greek islands begin to cool. Read Cyprus Guide.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which Greek island is the cheapest to fly to?

Crete, Rhodes, and Corfu are typically the cheapest to fly to from the UK due to the high volume of low-cost carriers serving their airports.

How much is a meal in Greece in 2026?

A main course in a traditional local taverna typically costs between €10 - €15. A filling 'Gyros' wrap remains the best budget option at under €5.

Is island-hopping expensive?

It can be if you use fast catamarans. Using traditional "slow" ferries and focusing on one island chain (like the Dodecanese) keeps moving costs affordable.
