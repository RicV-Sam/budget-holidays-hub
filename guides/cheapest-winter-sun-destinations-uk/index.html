Cheapest Winter Sun Destinations From UK (2026 Guide)

Last updated: March 2026

Winter Sun Summary

  • Best Short-Haul Value: Canary Islands (Tenerife/Lanzarote) remain the most reliable budget choice.
  • Best All-Inclusive Value: Antalya, Turkey offers 5-star luxury at low-season prices through November and March.
  • Best Medium-Haul Value: Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt provides guaranteed heat and world-class diving for under £500.
  • Top Budget Tip: Look for "shoulder season" weeks (early Dec or late Jan) for 30-50% savings compared to Christmas/New Year.

Human Experience Signal: Our team has tracked winter flight patterns from the UK for 5 years. In 2026, the real value lies in the "Canary Island Hack"—booking accommodation in local towns like Puerto de la Cruz instead of the major resort strips.

Escaping the UK winter doesn't have to cost a fortune. In 2026, high flight volume from UK regional airports to destinations like the Canary Islands and the Turkish Riviera has kept prices competitive. Tenerife vs Antalya: Which is Better for Winter?

If you're deciding between these two giants, it comes down to your holiday style. Tenerife offers more reliable "beach weather" and a massive choice of DIY apartments. Antalya, however, wins on "all-inclusive value"—you can often secure a 5-star hotel with all meals and drinks for under £450 per person in the winter.
