Best Areas to Stay in Portugal for Budget Travellers

Choosing the right neighbourhood is key to keeping your costs down in Portugal. For budget travellers, looking just outside the primary tourist hotspots can result in significant savings on accommodation. Areas that are well-connected by public transport hubs, such as the vibrant Lisbon, allow you to stay in more affordable guesthouses and hostels while still having easy access to all the major landmarks and attractions.

In Portugal, you will often find that neighbourhoods popular with students or locals offer a more authentic vibe alongside lower prices for both beds and meals. Consider exploring the historic Porto or the coastal Algarve, which frequently feature charming independent cafes, budget-friendly eateries, and a variety of low-cost lodging options ranging from boutique hostels to simple family-run hotels. By basing yourself in these vibrant districts, you not only save money but also get to experience a side of Portugal that many tourists miss.
