Cheapest Holidays Under £500 From UK (2026 Expert List)

Last updated: March 2026

The £500 Blueprint

  • The 'Package' Rule: For under £500, package deals (TUI/Jet2) often beat DIY bookings during shoulder seasons.
  • Top Beach Pick: The Algarve, Portugal consistently offers 7 nights + flights for under £450 in May/June.
  • Top City Pick: Krakow, Poland is the king of low-cost city breaks, easily achievable for under £300.
  • Booking Window: The "Magic Window" for £500 holidays is 8-12 weeks before departure.

Human Experience Signal: Don't just look for the cheapest headline price. A £300 holiday to a high-inflation city can end up costing more than a £450 all-inclusive break. We calculate the "Total Trip Cost" to ensure you stay under budget.

Is a week-long holiday for under £500 still possible in 2026? **The answer is yes**, but it requires a strategic approach to destination selection and booking timing. Our research shows that by focusing on high-volume routes and value-driven regions like the Costa Blanca or the Algarve, UK travellers can still enjoy a quality 7-night break without exceeding a £500 limit.
