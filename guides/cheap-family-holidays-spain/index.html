cheap family holidays spain

Last updated: April 2026

💡 Quick Answer

For cheap family holidays in Spain, look at Costa Blanca and Costa Dorada in May, June, or September. Family apartments plus low-cost flights usually beat all-inclusive peak-season pricing.

Cheap family holidays Spain searches usually come down to one question: package deal or self-catering? For many UK families, a two-bedroom apartment in a walkable resort with supermarket access is the biggest saver. For wider Spain planning first, check our main cheap Spain guide.

Cheapest Months for Family Spain Holidays

Families tied to school breaks pay a premium, so timing matters more than destination. If dates are flexible, these windows tend to offer the strongest value:

  • Best family value: May and early June
  • Second-best window: Mid to late September
  • Most expensive: Late July and August school holidays

Flights from UK for Family Trips

Manchester, Birmingham, Bristol, and London airports all have strong Spain frequencies in family seasons. Early-morning outbound flights can reduce delay risk and preserve your first holiday day, which adds value on shorter trips.

Best Family Destinations and Areas in Spain

  • Costa Blanca (Benidorm outskirts, Calpe): Great apartment supply and child-friendly beaches.
  • Costa Dorada (Salou, Cambrils): Good family hotels and easy day trips.
  • Majorca alternatives on mainland: Costa del Sol hubs often have better value transfers.
  • Tenerife south: Reliable weather for younger children outside peak heat.

Budget Tips for Families

  • Price apartment plus flights against all-inclusive packages; results can flip week by week.
  • Pick resorts with free splash areas, promenades, or playgrounds to cut paid activity spend.
  • Book airport transfer bundles early; family taxi fares can erase "cheap flight" savings.
  • Use one checked bag for family essentials and cabin bags for the rest.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest part of Spain for families?

Costa Blanca is usually one of the cheapest due to flight frequency and wide accommodation choice.

Is Spain cheaper than Greece for family holidays?

Spain often wins on flight price and package volume, while Greece can vary more by island.

Should families choose all-inclusive or self-catering?

Self-catering can be cheaper outside peak school periods; all-inclusive may win during high season promotions.

How can families cut food costs in Spain?

Use supermarkets for breakfast/snacks and book accommodation with a kitchenette.
