Cheap Holidays to Spain 2026: The Ultimate Value Rankings

Last updated: March 2026

⚡ Quick Decision: Where to go in Spain?

  • 📍 Cheapest Overall: Costa Blanca (Benidorm/Alicante) - 7 nights from £320.
  • 📍 Best for Families: Costa del Sol - Huge resort variety and short transfers.
  • 📍 Best Value "Authentic": Murcia - 20% cheaper than major tourist hubs.

💡 Quick Answer:

Yes. Spain remains the absolute king of volume and variety for UK travellers. With over 20 UK airports flying to 30+ Spanish destinations, the sheer competition keeps prices lower than any other Western European country. It is the best choice for flexibility and regional variety.

Spain is the undisputed benchmark for UK budget travel in 2026. Whether you are seeking a £250 city break in Valencia or a week-long family escape to the Costa Blanca, Spain's infrastructure is built for value. This guide breaks down the "Great Spanish Price Divide" to ensure you choose the right region for your budget. For a broader comparison, see how Spain ranks in our 2026 budget destination rankings.

Best areas to stay

  • Costa Blanca: The "Budget Leader." Benidorm and Alicante offer the highest density of affordable beds and low-cost flights in Europe.
  • Costa del Sol: Ideal for package deal volume. Focus on Fuengirola or Torremolinos for the best resort-to-price ratio.
  • Murcia (Costa Cálida): The "Hidden Gem." Often 20-30% cheaper than the neighbouring Costas, with a much more authentic Spanish feel.

Cheapest months to travel

Cheapest Months: January (City breaks) and May/June or Sept/Oct (Beach). Avoid August at all costs if you are on a strict budget.

Flights from UK departure airports

London Stansted and Manchester are the dominant hubs for Spanish flights. Using regional airports like East Midlands or Bristol can also yield savings of up to £50 on parking and travel time.

Budget tips (real, practical)

  • Search departures Tuesday to Thursday first, then compare against weekend fares.
  • Compare package totals with bags and transfers included before choosing DIY.
  • Book flexible-rate hotels and re-check prices 2-4 weeks before departure.
  • Use regional UK airports when transfer and parking costs offset ticket savings.

Who is Spain Best For?

Spain is the Top Choice for Families who need short flight times and safe beaches, and for First-Time Budget Travellers who want the security of well-established tourist infrastructure.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest place in Spain?

The Costa Blanca (specifically Benidorm) is consistently the cheapest holiday destination in Spain for UK travellers due to the massive supply of accommodation and high flight frequency.

Are holidays to Spain still cheap in 2026?

Yes, provided you avoid peak school holidays. Mid-week departures and shoulder-season bookings (May/June) remain some of the best travel value in the world from the UK.

Which UK airport is cheapest for Spain?

London Stansted is typically the price leader for flights to Spain, followed closely by London Luton and Manchester.

How can I find a "last minute" deal to Spain?

Check TUI and easyJet holidays approximately 10-14 days before you want to fly. This is when remaining package inventory is often slashed by up to 40%.

Is it cheaper to eat out or self-cater in Spain?

In 2026, "Menu del Dia" (Menu of the Day) lunches offer incredible value for eating out, often providing 3 courses and a drink for under £12.
