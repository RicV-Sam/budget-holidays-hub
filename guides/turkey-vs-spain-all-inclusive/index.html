Turkey vs Spain all-inclusive

Last updated: April 2026

💡 Quick Answer

Winner on pure all-inclusive value: Turkey. Turkey is usually cheaper for 4-star and 5-star all-inclusive packages and often offers bigger resorts for the money. Spain wins for shorter flights, easier airport access, and familiar resort logistics for UK families.

Side-by-side comparison table

Category Turkey Spain
Cost 7-night all-inclusive: approx. £360-£700 7-night all-inclusive: approx. £420-£780
Flights from UK Good major-airport coverage, longer flight times Very strong coverage including many regional UK airports

Best for Luxury-for-less, resort-focused breaks, families who stay onsite Short-haul convenience, mixed itinerary holidays, nightlife variety

Cost comparison

Turkey is often 10-20% cheaper for comparable all-inclusive standards, especially in Antalya and Dalaman. Spain can still deliver strong value, particularly in Costa Blanca and shoulder-season Balearic offers. Compare this page with Turkey all-inclusive deals and Spain all-inclusive deals.

Best destinations

  • Top Turkey picks: Antalya for large waterpark resorts, Dalaman for family-friendly all-inclusive zones.
  • Top Spain picks: Costa Blanca for lower prices, Costa del Sol for family convenience, Tenerife for year-round options.

For broader Spain planning, also check Spain main guide, Spain in June, Spain in September, family holidays in Spain, and cheapest places in Spain. For Turkey context, see cheap holidays to Turkey.

When to go

May, June, and September are usually the best-value months in both destinations. Turkey often has exceptional all-inclusive pricing in late spring and autumn. Spain has stronger availability for short-lead bookings, especially from regional UK airports.

Pros and cons

Turkey: pros and cons

  • Pros: Better 4-star/5-star value for all-inclusive packages.
  • Pros: Resort facilities are often bigger for the same budget.
  • Cons: Longer flight times from the UK.
  • Cons: Fewer ultra-short break options than Spain.

Spain: pros and cons

  • Pros: Easier UK airport access and shorter average flight times.
  • Pros: Great for mixed trips combining resort and local exploring.
  • Cons: Top all-inclusive resorts can cost more than Turkey equivalents.
  • Cons: Peak season crowding in popular Costas and islands.

Which should you choose?

Choose Turkey if you want maximum all-inclusive resort value and plan to spend most of your holiday onsite. Choose Spain if you want a shorter flight, more flexible airport options, and an easy balance of resort comfort with off-resort dining and exploring.

Frequently asked questions

Is Turkey cheaper than Spain for all-inclusive holidays?

Usually yes, especially at 4-star and 5-star level. Turkey often gives more resort facilities for the same spend.

Which has shorter flight times from the UK?

Spain. Most Spanish resorts are short haul, while Turkey is typically longer from UK airports.

Which is better for families?

Both are strong, but the decision depends on style: Turkey for bigger onsite resort value, Spain for easier travel logistics and flexibility.

Can Spain still beat Turkey on price?

Yes, especially with shoulder-season deals, regional-airport promotions, and lower-category all-inclusive packages.
