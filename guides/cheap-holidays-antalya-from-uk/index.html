Cheap Holidays to Antalya from the UK (2026 Guide)

Last updated: March 2026

💡 Quick Verdict: Is Antalya Worth It?

Yes. Antalya is the undisputed global champion for "Luxury for Less" in 2026. The high density of 5-star resorts creates fierce price competition, allowing UK travellers to secure all-inclusive packages that undercut the Eurozone by 30-40%.

Antalya is the gateway to the Turkish Riviera and a primary hub for UK budget sun-seekers. In 2026, the strength of the Pound against the Lira has made the "on-the-ground" costs incredibly low. Whether you want a high-end resort in Lara Beach or a boutique stay in the historic Old Town, Antalya delivers consistent value. For a full cost comparison, see our 2026 budget destination rankings.

Strategic Value: When and How to Book

Cheapest Months: April, May, and October. November is also excellent for "winter sun" if you focus on heated pool resorts. See our Winter Sun Pillar for details.

Cheapest UK Airports: London Stansted, Gatwick, and Manchester are the high-volume hubs. Jet2 and SunExpress often have the most competitive rates for Antalya (AYT).

Best Areas to Stay on a Budget

  • Kaleiçi (Old Town): The "Authority Choice" for culture. Stay in restored Ottoman mansions for under £40 a night and enjoy the best local "Lokanta" dining.
  • Konyaaltı: Modern and accessible. Perfect for budget apartments and easy access to the public beach and tram line.
  • Lara Beach: The hub for all-inclusive deals. While the front line is expensive, the streets just one block back offer incredible value.

Antalya vs Tenerife

If you're deciding on a shoulder-season break, Antalya usually offers cheaper 5-star luxury, while Tenerife offers more consistent beach weather. For a detailed breakdown, see our Tenerife vs Antalya comparison.

Better or Cheaper Alternatives?

  • Sharm El Sheikh: Often provides better winter sun value for Red Sea heat. Read Sharm Guide.
  • Dalamann/Marmaris: A slightly more 'British-focused' resort vibe which can sometimes yield lower package rates in peak summer.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Antalya cheaper than Greece?

Yes, in 2026, Antalya is consistently 20-35% cheaper than the Greek Islands for all-inclusive packages and dining out, largely due to the Lira exchange rate.

What is the cheapest month for Antalya?

October and May are the "Sweet Spot" months where flight frequency is high but resort prices drop significantly before and after the summer peak.

How much is a beer in Antalya?

In local bars in Kaleiçi, a large beer is typically £2.50 - £3.50, though prices in luxury 5-star resorts are significantly higher (unless included in your package).
