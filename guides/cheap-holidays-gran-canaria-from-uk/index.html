Best Areas to Stay in Gran Canaria for Budget Travellers

Choosing the right neighbourhood is key to keeping your costs down in Gran Canaria. For budget travellers, looking just outside the primary tourist hotspots can result in significant savings on accommodation. Areas that are well-connected by public transport hubs, such as the capital Las Palmas, allow you to stay in more affordable guesthouses and hostels while still having easy access to all the major landmarks and attractions.

In Gran Canaria, you will often find that neighbourhoods popular with students or locals offer a more authentic vibe alongside lower prices for both beds and meals. Consider exploring the bustling Playa del Inglés or the family-oriented Maspalomas, which frequently feature charming independent cafes, budget-friendly eateries, and a variety of low-cost lodging options ranging from boutique hostels to simple family-run hotels. By basing yourself in these vibrant districts, you not only save money but also get to experience a side of Gran Canaria that many tourists miss.
