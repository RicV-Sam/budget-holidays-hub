Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Check what's included—some 'all-inclusive' deals also include water sports
  • Look for hotels that offer a 'dine-around' program to sample local tavernas
  • Travel in a group to unlock better rates on larger family rooms or suites
  • Fly with low-cost carriers and book your own transfers to save more

Best Areas to Stay in Greece for Budget Travellers

When it comes to finding affordable lodging in Greece, location is everything. Savvy travellers often bypass the expensive city centres in favour of districts like the historic Plaka in Athens, where you can find much better value for your money. These neighbourhoods usually boast excellent transport links, making it a breeze to commute to the main sights while enjoying a quieter, more local environment at night.

Areas such as affordable stays in Crete and the diverse islands of Rhodes are particularly well-suited for those watching their pennies. Here, the concentration of budget-friendly hostels and simple apartments is higher, and the local dining scene reflects the more modest prices of the residents. Staying in these parts of Greece allows you to stretch your budget further, giving you more freedom to spend on experiences and attractions rather than just a place to sleep.
