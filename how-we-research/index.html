How We Research

Our Data-Driven Methodology for UK Travel Value

At Budget Holidays Hub, we believe that "budget travel" shouldn't mean guesswork. To ensure our guides are accurate and useful for UK travellers, we follow a rigorous, data-driven research methodology.

1. Live Price Monitoring

We don't rely on outdated brochures or generic "starting from" prices. Our team performs weekly price checks across major UK travel platforms, including:

  • Airlines: Ryanair, easyJet, Jet2, Wizz Air, and British Airways.
  • Accommodation: Booking.com, Airbnb, and direct hotel portals.
  • Package Providers: TUI, Loveholidays, and On the Beach.

2. Real-World Cost Analysis

A "cheap" flight is only the beginning. We calculate the Total Trip Cost by researching local inflation and spending data in each destination:

  • The 'Pint & Pizza' Index: Tracking the cost of basic meals and drinks in tourist vs. local areas.
  • Transport Logistics: Comparing airport transfer costs (train vs. bus vs. taxi).
  • Hidden Fees: Identifying city taxes, resort fees, and luggage surcharges that impact the final price.

3. Seasonal Trend Tracking

Holiday prices fluctuate wildly based on UK school calendars and local events. We analyze historical pricing data to identify the "Sweet Spot" for booking each destination—the precise window where flight volume is high but demand is manageable.

4. "Boots on the Ground" Insight

Wherever possible, we supplement our data with real-world feedback from travellers and local experts. This helps us identify when a "budget" area has become overpriced or when a new "hidden gem" offers superior value.

5. Independence & Integrity

Our research is entirely independent. We do not accept payment from tourism boards or hotels to be featured in our "Best Value" rankings. If a destination is on our list, it's there because the data proves it offers genuine value for UK holidaymakers.
