Affiliate Disclosure

How We Support Our Research

Budget Holidays Hub is an independent UK travel research site. To provide our guides for free and maintain our detailed research into budget travel trends, we sometimes earn a commission when you click on links and make a booking.

How It Works

When you click on a link in our guides to a booking platform (such as Booking.com, TUI, or Loveholidays) and complete a transaction, we may receive a small affiliate commission from the provider. This commission is paid by the company, not by you.

It Costs You Nothing Extra

The commission we earn has no impact on the price you pay. In many cases, our research and the timing of your booking will save you significantly more than the commission we receive. Whether you use our links or go directly to the booking site, the price remains the same.

Maintaining Editorial Independence

Our editorial standards are strictly separated from our commercial relationships. We only link to providers that our research shows offer genuine value and reliable service to UK travellers. A commission payment never influences our "Best Value" rankings or the objectivity of our destination reviews.

Our Commitment to You

Our priority is always the traveller. We are committed to transparency and to providing the most accurate, useful, and unbiased budget travel advice available to UK holidaymakers.

Thank you for supporting Budget Holidays Hub by using our affiliate links, which allows us to keep our expert research and guides accessible to everyone.
